Easy return

Customer has to pay returns charges.

Upon receipt of the package, if the item does not suit you you can go to the site in the space MY ACCOUNT / MY RETURN and complete the return form. Then it will print it and attach it to your package. The package must be returned at your expense to our warehouse. Upon receipt of the package, if the product conforms to the sale then you will be refunded.

If the product is faulty, we invite you to contact the customer service (store.rg@fft.fr) in order to obtain a free return voucher.

To obtain an exchange, we invite you to return the item and place a new order. Once we receive your product we will proceed its refund.

You have a delay of 14 days to return your product to the receipt of the item to your shipping address.
